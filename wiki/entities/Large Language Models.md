# Large Language Models (LLMs)

Large Language Models are artificial intelligence systems capable of understanding and generating human language. They use transformer architectures and attention mechanisms to process context and generate coherent text.

## Applications
- Content generation
- [[Chatbots]]
- Code generation
- Data analysis

## Challenges
- [[Hallucinations]]
- Bias
- Computational cost
- Ethics
